About portfolio:

The first part of this portfolio (UCSD Coding Bootcamp Projects) is composed of projects that were completed during the course of the coding bootcamp program I completed at UC San Diego in 2018. These are very basic projects that were a great learning process in working with the new technologies I had learned. They include Bootstrap, jQuery, MySQL, MongoDB, Express.js, Node.js, and React.js and many others.

The second part of this portfolio (personal projects) is composed of projects that I have taken on to create. I like to implement the skills I've learned in multiple ways in order to achieve as much mastery as possible. I believe that hard work and practice is the key to mastery of anything. These projects have let me practice the skills I have learned and be creative.
